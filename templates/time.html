Meanwhile Catch a Poem


In the hush of night, where shadows play, A bus stop waits, in soft dismay. Beneath the glow of neon light, Silent whispers fill the night. Benches stand, a quiet row, Reflecting dreams of those who go. Leaves dance gently in the breeze, As time drips slowly, like the trees. A lone trash can, weary and worn, Holds secrets of the day, now shorn. The city breathes, a tranquil sigh, Under the watchful, starry sky. In this moment, still and clear, Life pauses, drawing near. A fleeting glimpse of stories untold, In the heart of night, where dreams unfold.
